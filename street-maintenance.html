Street Maintenance

Everyone knows that daily wear and tear slowly deteriorates our streets. Now imagine you live in the fastest growing county in Missouri. Ozark is booming! But with growth comes challenges.

Everyone wants their street to be well maintained, but in a rapidly growing city that is easier said than done. The city maintains an evolving list of streets in need of repair and addresses each street based upon its condition and funding.

The bad news is even if your street is in great shape today it could be several years before it is scheduled to be replaced, and by then you could be dodging potholes on your way to the shops. But don't worry, good news could be coming soon. Pass this transportation tax and the city's 15 year street maintenance plan gets cut to just 5 years!

Benefits

  • 15 year plan, cut to 5 years
  • Increase $ for city streets
  • Beautify our city
  • Increase economic opportunity
  • Avoid good streets becoming bad
  • Vechicle and pedestrian safety
