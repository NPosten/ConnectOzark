3/8 Cent Transportation Sales Tax

Revenue from this tax can only be used to fund transportation and pedestrian projects.

The tax will sunset after 5 years unless voters elect to renew it.

The city estimates we will raise approximately $5.5M over five years.

Project Fund Allocation

How much will this cost me?

If you spend $100 per week on taxable goods:

  • $0.38 per week
  • $19.50 per year
