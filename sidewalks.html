Sidewalks

Creating a pedestrian friendly city has benefits that will be felt throughout the community

Children will have safe routes to schools and residents will have safe routes to shopping, dining, and other businesses.

Walkable cities promote healthy communities and provide significant economical benefits. Safe, well-maintained sidewalks have been shown to increase property values and promote tourism.

In addition to safety, health, and economic benefits - walkable cities provide opportunites for resident to stay socially connected, strenthening our community bonds.

Benefits

  • Improve safety
  • Promote healthy activity
  • Increase property values
  • Improve access to schools
  • Improve access to businesses
  • Enhanced sense of community
  • Promote tourism

Project

New sidewalks will connect residential areas with a vibrant area of schools and businesses along Ozark's South Street corridor. Kids will have a safe path to school including a new crosswalk connection to South Elementary. Residents will be benefit from safe, walkable access to shopping and dinning.
