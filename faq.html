Frequently Asked Questions

How much will this cost me?

It will cost $0.38 for every $100 you spend on taxable goods. If you spent $100 a week for an entire year, it would only cost $19.50.

How much revenue will the tax generate?

The city estimates it will raise an additional $1.1 million each year, for a total of $5.5M over the span of the tax period.

How long will the transportation tax be in effect?

The tax will sunset in five years, unless the voters decided to renew it.

What if the tax doesn't pass?

  • Federal and state funds go to some other city if we don't have the revenue to cover our share.
  • Intersection become more congested and less safe.
  • Riverside Bridge will likely remain one-lane and may stay closed for quite awhile.
  • Sidewalk and trail projects stop or are scaled down.
